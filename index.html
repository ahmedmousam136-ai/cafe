Our Story

Where Bengali
Heritage Meets
Modern Craft

Born in the heart of Kolkata, Āroma is a tribute to the timeless Bengali tradition of adda — meaningful conversations over cups of perfectly brewed coffee. We blend artisanal techniques with local flavors, creating an experience that honors our roots while embracing contemporary elegance.
